Ghazal

jaane se pehle hame kuch btaya to hota

bin tumhare jiyen ge kaise ye sikhaya to hota

abdi he ye musaafat ya aarzi he ye judaai

braheraasat na hi btaate, kuch ishara dikhaya to hota.

(Anas Kashf)

Ghazal

dil ki gali veran ho gai

wo jise chahte the hum, anjan ho gai

ab to zamane me hmari izzat bhi na rhi

nakam aashiq hmari pehchan ho gai.

(Anas Kashif)

Ghazal

na Ma'aloom aisi keya khta ho gai

ke wo upar bhar ki saza de gya

lgta to tha ke wafa de ga hme

de kr to gya mgr jfa de gya

jis ki badolat mere dil ki khil jaati thi kali

wahi dill ko abdi khazan de gya

mere liye to sab kuch wo hi tha

jaate jaate mera to jahan le gya

is ke bin to zindagi ho gai he adhoori

a mere khuda! Tu use kahaan le gya?

(Anas Kashif)
